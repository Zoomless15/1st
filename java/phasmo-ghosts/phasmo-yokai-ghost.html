Yokai:

EVIDENCE: Spirit box, Ghost Orb, Dots

Hunt Threshold: 50% (80% When someone talks close to them)

Speed: 1.7m/s (Normal increase)

Hidden Ability: -

Give Away:Unable to find you during a hunt despite active electric devices

Exclude: -

Hunt: Yokais cannot hear voices or notice electrical devices unless they are in it's direct vicinity (2-3m). Talking within 2-3m of them may trigger a hunt.

Trivia

In Japanese Folklore, Yokai (妖怪, or "strange apparition") is a term broadly encompassing unexplained phenomenons, supernatural apparitions or spirits, which can take a wide variety of forms going from animals to inanimate objects.

Lore

„Yokai are common ghosts that are attracted to human voices. They can usually be found haunting family homes.”

—The Journal
