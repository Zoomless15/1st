Trivia

The moroi (plural idem) are a type of vampires in Romanian folklore who practiced vampirism while being still alive (as opposed to strigoi who became vampires after death). In modern usage, moroi simply means "ghost" in Romanian. The Moroi is the ghost with the fastest potential speed in the game.

Lore

„Moroi have risen from the grave to drain energy from the living. They have been known to place curses on their victims, curable only by antidotes or moving very far away.”

—The Journal
