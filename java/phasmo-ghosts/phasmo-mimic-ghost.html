The Mimic:

EVIDENCE: Spirit Box, Fingerprints, Thermometer

Hunt Threshold:Depends on mimiced Ghost

Speed:Based jon the Ghost it Mimics

Hidden Ability: Can copy the behaviour of every other ghost

Give Away:3 Evidences on Nightmare, 4 on Professional (One of which is Ghost Orbs)

Exclude: No Ghost Orb Evidence

Ability : Always produces Ghost Orb Evidence, no matter what the journal says

Trivia

The Mimic is the only ghost that can leave handprints on the Exit Door: it must be mimicking a Yurei, it must use the door-closing ability on the exit door, and the ultraviolet evidence is not hidden. Hence, this occurrence is very rare.

Lore

„The Mimic is an elusive, mysterious, copycat ghost that mirrors traits and behaviours from others, including other ghost types.”

—The Journal
