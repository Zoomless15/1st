Thaye:

EVIDENCE: Ghost Orb, Ghost Writing, DOTS

Hunt Threshold:75% - 15% (-6% per aging step)

Speed:2.75m/s (young) -0.175 (per increment) 1m/s (max old)

Hidden Ability: Constantly Ages Starts hyperactive and fast, becomes slower and less active over time

Give Away: -

Exclude: Low Activity / Slow Hunt Speed early on

Ability : Every 2 Minutes the ghost attempts to age when you are close, if not the timer will be increased by 30 seconds. Be in the ghost room to make him age.

Trivia

Thaye (IPA: /θəjɛ́/, pronounced "thuh-yeh") are also known as sarai and tasei in Burmese (written သရဲ) are disembodied spirits in Myanmar folklore that have many forms at once, depending on the beholder. They come in the deep of the night and cause minor nuisances. When The Mimic mimics a Thaye, it will choose a random age.

Lore

„Thaye have been known to rapidly age over time, even in the afterlife. From what we've learned, they seem to deteriorate faster while within the presence of the living.”

—The Journal
