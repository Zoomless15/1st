Spirit:

EVIDENCE: EMF 5, Spirit box, Writing

Hunt Threshold: 50%

Speed: 1.7m/s (NORMAL Speed)

Hunts before 3 Minutes are up after previously being smudged

Hidden Ability: Smudging the Room / Ghost prevents hunts for 3 Minutes (rather than the normal 1.5 Minutes)

Giveaway: -

Exclude: Hunts before 3 Minutes are up after previously being smudged

When on very low Sanity, Smudge the Ghost during a hunt and run a timer. If it takes around 3 Minutes for a new hunt, probably a Spirit

Trivia

Although the journal says that the Spirit is the most common ghost, all ghost types actually have the same chance of getting selected by the game. Perhaps this description was thought to mean that the Spirit is the "basic" ghost type other types were based upon in the process of development. The Spirit is the first ghost in the list and is automatically selected at the start of each contract by default.
