Trivia

In Japanese mythology, Raijus (雷獣, or "thunder beasts") are divine creatures most commonly depicted as a canine animal surrounded by lightning. Raijus are thought to be living in the sky, descending onto earth during thunderstorms by riding a lightning bolt to cause mayhem and destruction. As such, lightning strikes were often interpreted as the passage of a Raiju or divine punishment in ancient Japan.
