Trivia

Yūrei are Japanese folklore ghosts known for haunting a specific location at a specific time (generally corresponding to their time of death). They are famous for having an appearance which strikes fear into their foes, often resulting in self-mutilation or suicide. The Yurei is commonly regarded as a very difficult ghost to identify, along with the Goryo, due to its lack of hunting abilities and/or lack of recognizable abilities. The Yurei, though regarded as having a "stronger effect on people's sanity", does not increase the passive sanity drain of players, nor does it have an increased drain from pseudo-line of sight like with the Phantom. Furthermore, the Jinn's ability to drain 25% sanity rather than the Yurei's meager 15% while the Jinn has other abilities ironically makes the Yurei weak when juxtaposed to other ghosts.
