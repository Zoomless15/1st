Deogen:

EVIDENCE: Spirit Box, Ghost Writing, DOTS

Hunt Threshold: 40%

Speed:3m/s (more than 2.5m away) 0.4m/s (less than 2.5m away)

Hidden Ability: Using the Spirit Box within 1m of him, will make him respond with a heavy breathing sound

Give Away: GIGA FAST when you are hidden, omega slow when next to him

Exclude: -

Ability : You CANNOT HIDE from this ghost, you will need to loop or kite it in the open. Despite what some people have you believe, Deogens DO NOT enforce Spirit Box on Nightmare.

Trivia

"Deogen" is a ghost from the Flemish (Belgian) late 18-century tale called De Ogen (Dutch for "the eyes", pronounced /de'oːɣən/). The tale is about a strange yellow-eyed fog haunting the Sonian Forest which likes to stalk its victims at night, moving slowly when being looked at and fast while being turned away from. Because the Deogen always has line-of-sight with all players, most "safe spots" that take advantage of a lack of line-of-sight will not work, resulting in death. In fact, this may also result in the Deogen killing players through walls that are sufficiently thin.

Lore

„Sometimes surrounded by an endless fog, Deogen have been eluding ghost hunters for years. These ghosts have been reported to find even the most hidden prey, before stalking them into exhaustion.”
