Demon:

EVIDENCE: Fingerprints, Ghost Writing, Thermometer

Hunt Threshold: 70% (100% Ability)

Speed: 1.7m/s (Normal increase)

Hidden Ability: May (regardless of Sanity) walk towards a random player, then initiate a hunt Crucifixes have 5m Radius

Give Away: -

Exclude: -

Hunt:Somehow all the Crucifixes are gone. Smudging them only prevents hunts for 1 Minute.

Trivia

On 42 Edgefield Road, if the Demon is in the Garage, Master Bedroom, or Hallway, it is able to trigger crucifixes that are sitting in the van, as their increased effective range causes them to reach towards the back of these rooms, where the ghost may happen to wander to when it tries to hunt. This can also happen with any ghost, but will be less common. In mythology, demons tend to have horrifying forms, being commonly depicted with horns, tails, claws, and other inhuman appendages. In-game, Demons draw from the regular pool of Ghost Models without any sort of modifications.
