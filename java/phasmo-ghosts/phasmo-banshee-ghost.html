Banshee:

EVIDENCE:Fingerprints, Ghost Orb, Dots

Hunt Threshold 50% (only target sanity)

Speed: 1.7m/s (NORMAL Speed)

Hidden Ability: Prefers Singing Ghost Events

Give Away: MP:Ignores closer players during a Hunt and focuses on the selected target Banshee Scream on Paramic

Exclude: -

Hunt: Banshee will pic a random target, and only go for them during a hunt. It will also only check the target's Sanity for the Hunt threshhold. If it's target is outside the Investigation Area, the Banshee will act like a normal ghost.

Trivia

The Banshee is a female spirit in Irish folklore who heralds the death of a family member, usually by wailing or shrieking. A Banshee translates to "woman of the fairy mound" or "fairy woman" in its roots from Ireland. In Phasmophobia, Banshees can be either female or male, as the ghost's gender is randomly chosen.
