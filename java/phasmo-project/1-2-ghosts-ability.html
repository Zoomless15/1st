Welcome to Phasmophobia Quick Guide

Banshee: Prefers Singing Ghost Events

Demon: May (regardless of Sanity) walk towards a random player, then initiate a hunt Crucifixes have 5m Radius

Deogen: Using the Spirit Box within 1m of him, will make him respond with a heavy breathing sound

Goryo: DOTS can't be seen without camera

Hantu: Cold Breath on Ghost during Hunt

Jinn: May reduce the Sanity of nearby players by 25% IF the Breaker is on

Mare: May sometimes turn off a light immediately after a player turns it on

Mimic: Can copy the behaviour of every other ghost

Moroi: Hunt speed depends on player sanity when the hunt starts

Myling: Higher Chance for Singing Ghost Events Hunt: Footstep Sound range is lower than Flashlight flicker range

Obake: Only has a 75% chance to leave Fingerprints for an interaction

Oni: Flickers less during Hunts. Drains sanity during Ghost Events at twice the speed

Onryo: 3 firelights each blown out 1 time, or any other combination will trigger a hunt

Phantom: Reduces Sanity when you look at it

Poltergeist: Can throw more than one object occasionally

Raiju: Faster near electronics

Revenant: When it senses a player or their equipment: Very Fast; When it sees no player or reaches last known player position: Really Slow

Shade: Unable to do Throws, Ghost Events or Hunts when another player is in a room with her. Can Appear as Black Shadow in Summoning Circle

Spirit: Smudging the Room / Ghost prevents hunts for 3 Minutes (rather than the normal 1.5 Minutes)

Thaye:Constantly Ages. Starts hyperactive and fast, becomes slower and less active over time

The Twins: Two interactions back to back in different rooms

Wraith: Can teleport to players and trigger EMF 2

Yokai: Unable to find you during a hunt despite active electric devices

Yurei: Ability that reduces the Sanity of nearby players (Door Shut) More likely to do Mistball Ghost Events
