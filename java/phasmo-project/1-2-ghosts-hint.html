Welcome to Phasmophobia Quick Guide

Banshee: Pfavours the singing and humming vocalizations for events and interactions. They also have a unique noise on the sound mic (rarely happens).

Demon: is the only ghost that can hunt at 100% sanity. Crucifixes have 5m Radius

Deogen: can find players anywhere on the map

Goryo: is a homebody and rarely leaves it's starting spot. Dots is always an evidence type.

Hantu: breath should be visible during a hunt (might be patched out atm). Freezing is always an evidence type.

Jinn: can't turn off breaker

Mare: can’t turn on a light

Mimic:has 3 evidence on nightmare

Moroi: Being cursed by the Moroi, caused by a response on the Parabolic microphone or Spirit Box, causes the player to double their passive sanity drain, and lights will not negate this sanity drain.

Myling: Hfootsteps are extremely quiet during a hunt.

Obake:can leave 6 fingers and can sometimes not leave fingies.

Oni: angry when you close to it, Polter amount of activity

Onryo: 3 firelights each blown out 1 time, or any other combination will trigger a hunt

Phantom: disappear when you take a photo and when they’re hunting, they’ll be less visable and show itself for only 1 second and be invisible for like 5

Poltergeist: can throw mutiple objects at once and are the most active ghost other then onis

Raiju: extra fast around electrical equipment

Revenant: if they have line of sight on you, they are twice as fast, but if not, they are twice as slow. If you are hiding listen to the footsteps because the revenants slow steps are extremely noticeable!

Shade: Very shy when you're in the same room as them. Also lowest hunt range.

Spirit: Smudging the Room / Ghost prevents hunts for 3 Minutes (rather than the normal 1.5 Minutes)

Thaye:Constantly Ages. Starts hyperactive and fast, becomes slower and less active over time

The Twins: Two interactions back to back in different rooms

Wraith: Can teleport to players and trigger EMF 2

Yokai: can hunt at 90% sanity if you are talking near the ghost

Yurei: Ability that reduces the Sanity of nearby players (Door Shut) More likely to do Mistball Ghost Events
